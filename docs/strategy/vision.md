# Product Vision

**Status:** Draft
**Last updated:** 2026-03-20

## What We're Building

A school-parent communication platform targeting pre-schools in Spain.
Modeled on tools like KinderClose but designed to win the Spanish market.

## Problem

Pre-school teachers spend significant time on informal, fragmented communication
with parents (WhatsApp groups, paper notes, verbal updates at pickup).
Parents lack real-time visibility into their child's day.
Schools have no unified tool that handles communication, daily reports,
attendance, and media sharing in one place.

## Solution

A web + mobile platform (iOS, Android, web) that:
- Connects teachers and parents through structured, private channels
- Replaces WhatsApp groups with a purpose-built, GDPR-compliant tool
- Gives parents daily visibility: activities, meals, naps, photos
- Gives schools a professional communication layer

## Target Market

- **Geography:** Spain (initial), with expansion potential to LATAM
- **Segment:** Private and semi-private pre-schools (0–6 years)
- **Buyer:** School director / administrator (pays the subscription)
- **End users:** Teachers and parents
